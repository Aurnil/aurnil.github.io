Intel Core i3-14100

The Intel Core i3-14100 is an excellent entry-level chip offering smooth everyday performance and light gaming capabilities. It supports both DDR4 and DDR5 RAM for flexible budget builds.

AMD Ryzen 5 7500F

The Ryzen 5 7500F is one of the standout budget CPUs of 2025. With 6 cores and 12 threads, it delivers impressive performance for gaming, content creation, and multitasking. Though it lacks integrated graphics, it makes up for it with a 5.0 GHz boost and excellent efficiency.
